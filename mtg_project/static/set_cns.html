Conspiracy

All new cards in Conspiracy depict the environment of the Paliano, the High City on the plane of Fiora, which is the home of Dack Fayden and the setting of the Magic comic book published by IDW. Conspiracy is a special large set with 197 cards. It was released on June 6, 2014.
